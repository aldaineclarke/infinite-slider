Krookodile

This Pokémon is known as the Bully of the Sands. Krookodile's mighty jaws can bite through heavy plates of iron with almost no effort at all.

Milotic

Milotic has provided inspiration to many artists. It has even been referred to as the most beautiful Pokémon of all.

Dusknior

At the bidding of transmissions from the spirit world, it steals people and Pokémon away. No one knows whether it has a will of its own.

Ninetails

A deity resides in the snowy mountains where this Pokémon lives. In ancient times, it was worshiped as that deity's incarnation.

Sceptile

Sceptile has seeds growing on its back. They are said to be bursting with nutrients that revitalize trees. This Pokémon raises the trees in a forest with loving care.

Staraptor

It never stops attacking even if it is injured. It fusses over the shape of its comb.
